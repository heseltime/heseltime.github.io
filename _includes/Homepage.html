Hello, I'm Jack Heseltine and this is HESELTIME
I am a formally trained Software and Cloud Engineer in love a professional working relationship with Enterprise Content Management (ECM).
I have experience across industries and believe in service to my community, developing my super-regional branch of the Red Cross, through professional-grade volunteer work in Upper Austria.

My day job is with FAW GMBH (LLC., a university spin-out customizing the Alfresco ECM system), in Hagenberg, Upper Austria, where I do ECM research and research-based support of our client teams like module development, containerization and upgrades.

I am always open to discuss positions, including contract ones, that can draw on my ECM experience and interests: I am mostly interested in remote work with some availability for travel (hybrid models) in the US or EU/UK, but require visa support elsewhere.

At the Linz General Hospital Blood and Tissue Bank (Red Cross) I am concentrating on a software engineering research project in the co-domains ECM and Hyper-Automation, in cooperation with Fachhochschule Oberoesterreich, Campus Hagenberg.

I formally worked for Red Cross in Linz throughout the Covid-19 pandemic from 2020-23 and continue to stay involved through this project.
